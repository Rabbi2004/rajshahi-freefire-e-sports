About Our Team

We Are The Champions

Rajshahi Free Fire is a professional e-sports team based in Rajshahi, Bangladesh. We specialize in competitive Free Fire gameplay and participate in paid and weekly series tournaments.

Our team consists of highly skilled players who are dedicated to excellence in gaming. We train daily, analyze strategies, and work as a unit to dominate the battlefield.

Whether you are a fan or a potential sponsor, we invite you to be a part of our exciting journey into the world of competitive gaming.
